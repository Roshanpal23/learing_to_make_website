Dish 1 Recipe

Ingredient:Udad dall,1/3 of rice,green chili,ginger,curry leaves,oil sock the udad daal and rice for 8-10hrs.and fine grind them in the grinder (or stone grinder for the traditional preparation). Add 2-3 green chilies,ginger,curry leaves,coriander leaves with daal and make fine and thick paste out of it.
